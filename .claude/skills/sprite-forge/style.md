GLOBAL STYLE — "Bronze Relief / Bio-mechanical" (Resonance Grid)

Render the subject in a single, consistent art style:

MATERIAL: sculpted cast bronze (warm, with oxidized teal-green patina settled into crevices) fused with polished black obsidian / onyx. Surfaces carry fine engraved Adinkra / ritual glyphs and selective gold-leaf inlay accents. The object is bio-mechanical — part ancient ritual artifact, part precision machine.

LIGHTING: one dramatic warm key light from the top-left, cool ambient fill, deep contact shadows in the engravings. Include small EMISSIVE glowing accents (cores, edge channels) in warm gold / cyan so an additive bloom pass enhances them. Keep high micro-contrast so the form reads clearly even when shrunk small.

PERSPECTIVE: consistent top-down 3/4 view with a slight tilt. Orthographic feel — no wide-angle or vanishing-point distortion.

FRAMING: a SINGLE subject, centered, filling about 85% of a SQUARE frame on a FULLY TRANSPARENT background (alpha). Absolutely no background scenery, no ground plane, no cast drop-shadow on a floor, no text, no labels, no UI, no framing border, no color swatches.

SILHOUETTE: prioritize a bold, instantly readable silhouette. Avoid thin filigree or fine detail that would turn to mush below ~40px.

DIRECTION: if the subject is directional (a vehicle, a creature with a clear front), orient its "front" pointing to the RIGHT of the frame (+X / east).
